Mission

Our mission here at Ballantyne Beans is to create the best cup of coffee and the best environment for our customers. Whether you are taking something to go or spending hours coding in our cafe, we are here to serve you and keep you going.

Background

Ballantyne Beans was started as a school project. As a bit of a coffee snob, I saw a need for a good local coffee shop in the Ballantyne area. There are some major chains but few small business options available, and fewer who are as obsessed with coffee quality as I am.
